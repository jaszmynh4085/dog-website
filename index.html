Dog Breeds

BREEDS:


339 breeds of dogs which are divided into groups based upon the dog’s purpose or function.

HERDING:


Examples:



Examples:


German shepherds are lareg dogs that can live up to 10 years. females weigh 50-70 pounds and are 22-24 inches tall. Males can weigh up to 65-90 pounds and are 24-26 inches tall.


Shelties, otherwise known as The Shetland Sheepdog, are small dogs that can live up to 14 years. They weigh around an average of 15-25 pounds and measure around 13-16 inches tall.


The Bearded Collie, otherwise known as Beardie, are medium to large dogs that can live up to 14 years. on average they weigh around 45-55 pounds. A female Beardie can measure up to 20-21 inches tall, while a male can measure up to 21-22 inches tall.



WORKING:


Examples:


German pinschers are medium dogs that can live up to 14 years. They weigh around 25-45 pounds and measure 17-20 inches in height.


Boxers are large dogs that can live up to 12 years. Males weigh 65-80 pounds and measure 23-25 inches in height while females weigh 15 pounds less than males and measure 21.5-23.5 inches in height.


Boerboels are extra large dogs that live up to 11 years. On adverage they weigh 150-20 pounds. Males measure 24-27 inches in height while females measure 22-25 inches in height.



TOY GROUP:


Examples:


Silky Terriers are miniature dogs that can live up to 15 years. on average they weigh around 10 pounds and are 9-10 inches tall.


Chihuahuas are minature dogs that can live up to 16 years. they weigh at most 6 pounts and are 5-8 inches tall.


Malteses are miniature dogs that can live up to 15 years. they weigh under 7 pounds and are 7-9 inches tall.



TERRIER:


Examples:


The Manchester Terrier is a small dog that can live up to 17 years. On average they weigh 12-22 pounds and are 15-16 inches tall


The Australian Terrier is a small dog that can live up to 15 years. On average they weigh 15-20 pounds and are 10-11 inches tall.


The Border Terrier is a small dog that can live up to 15 years. Females weigh 11.5-14 pounds while males weigh 13-15.5 pounds. On average they are 12-15 inches tall.



SPORTING:


Examples:


The American Water Spaniel are small to medium dogs that can live up to 14 years. Males are 30-45 pounds while females are 25-40 pounds. On average they measure 15-18 inches in height.


The Cocker Spaniel are small dogs that can live up to 14 years. The males weight 25-30 pounds and measure 14.5-15.5 inches in height, while the females weight 20-15 pounds and measure 13.5-14.5 inches in height.


The Flat Coated Retriever are large dogs that can live up to 10 years. on average they weigh 60-70 pounds. Males measure 23-24.5 inches in height while females measure 22-23.5 inches in height.



NON-SPORTING:


Examples:


Bulldogs are medium sized dogs that can live up to 10 years. Males weigh 50 pounds while females weigh 40 pounds. On average they are 14-15 inches tall.


The American Eskimo is a small dog that can live up to 15 years. Their standard weight and size are 23-25 pounds and 15-inches. They can be found in smaller sizes.


Dalmatians are medium to large dogs that can live up to 13 years. On average they weigh 45-70 pounds and are 19-24 inches tall.



HOUND:


Examples:


Bloodhounds are large dogs that can live up to 12 years. On average they weigh 90-110 pounds. Males measure 15-17 inches in heigh while females measure 32-25 inches in height.


Basset Hounds are medium to large dogs that can live up to 13 years. On average they weigh 40-65 pounds and measure 15 inches in heigh.


Beagles are small dogs that can live up to 15 years. On average they weigh 20-10 pounds and measure 13-15 inches in height. They can also be found as a smaller size and weigh under 20 pounds when they are 13 inches in height or under.

FUN FACTS:

*All sizes depends on who you ask.*


*Some places have medium/large dogs starting at 55 pounds others at 50 pounds and small/medium at 25-35 pounds*


For a dog to be considered a large dog breed they have to weigh at least 50 pounds and have a height of (around) 24 inches. From there breeders separate the dogs further. Dogs over 100 pounds will be considered giant dogs. Officially any dog that weighs more than 50 pounds but less than 100 are large dogs


Small to Medium sized dogs are about 18-22 inches in height and weigh about 35-65 (or 40-60) pounds


Small dog breeds may be around 10 inches. Then there are miniature dog breeds such as toy dogs that vary between 4 pounds and 12 pounds and teacup dogs that are smaller than usual toy dogs
